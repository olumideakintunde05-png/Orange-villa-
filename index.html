✦ Our Story

Where Every Stay Becomes a Memory

Nestled in the serene surroundings of Ifo, Ogun State, Orange Villa Hotel is a sanctuary of elegance and warmth. We believe that luxury should never come at the cost of comfort or your hard-earned money — and we've built our entire brand around that promise.

Whether you're a couple seeking a romantic retreat, a business traveler who demands reliability, or someone simply looking for a peaceful weekend escape, our hotel is your home away from home. Every corner of Orange Villa has been thoughtfully designed to soothe your senses and refresh your spirit.

We pride ourselves on impeccable cleanliness, round-the-clock security, and a warm, attentive staff who treat every guest like royalty. At Orange Villa, you're not just checking in — you're arriving somewhere you truly belong.
